Help Page

Sample Statistics

The Sample Statistics tab is designed to produce a boxplot displaying peak scores for both Meg3-KO and Meg3-PRO cell lines. Plots can be generated for both Proliferation and Differentiation timepoints using their respective radio buttons. Generation of the plot may be execute via the "Create Boxplott" button. Values indicating quartiles, medians, as well as minimums or maximums can be visualized by hovering your mouse over the generated plot. To zoom in you may use the plus and minus buttons in the top right of the plot, or you may also click and drag your mouse over the section of your choice. The generated plot may be saved as a PNG by selecting the camera button located in the top right of the plot. Pan, Autoscale, and Reset Axes functions may also be found in this location.

Search Peaks

Peak Table Inputs:

The Search Peaks tab can be utilized to generate a table of peaks from a defined timepoint, chromosome, and location on that chromosome. To begin, select a timepoint (proliferation or differentiation) using the radio buttons and proceed by selecting the cell line of your choice. After selecting your desired chromosome from the dropdown menu, you may use the slider on the screen to define your desired location on the chromosome for which you would like see data for. Upon selection of these parameters, the "Produce Summary Statistics" button may be used to generate your desired table.


Explore Peaks in Genome Browser:

With the criteria selected to generate your table of summary statistics, you may explore peaks in the genome browser and focus on a specific replicate by making your selection with the radio buttons. To visualize your peaks in the genome browser you may use the "Produce IGV" button. The curated data table may also be downloaded to your local device via the "Download CSV" button.

Gene Summary

The Gene Summary tab is designed to create a table of summary information for each gene. Results can be filtered by tiempoint (proliferation or differentiation) as well as cell line (Meg3-PRO and Meg3-KO) using their respective radio buttons. Results displayed in the data table can be sorted by Symbol, Chromosome, Gene Set, Gene Start and Stop Coordinates, Number of Peaks, as well as Fold Enrichment. Upon selecting your desired filtering criteria, results can be displayed by selecting the "Produce Gene and Peak Overlap Table" button. The curated data table may also be downloaded to your local device via the "Download CSV" button.

Peak Visualization

Plot Peaks:

The Peak Visualization tab can be utilized to observe signaling values for differentiation in a chromosome of your choice. Both Timepoint and Cell line may be selected for using their respective radio buttons, while Chromsomes may be specified via the dropdown menu. Visualization of your plot can be achieved by selecting the "Plot Peaks" button. To zoom in you may use the plus and minus buttons in the top right of the plot, or you may also click and drag your mouse over the section of your choice. The generated plot may be saved as a PNG by selecting the camera button located in the top right of the plot. Pan, Autoscale, and Reset Axes functions may also be found in this location.
