Current Research:

Meg3

Meg3, or Maternally expressed gene 3, encodes a long non-coding RNA. Meg3 is located in proximity to the Dlk1-Dio3 ncRNA locus, which is a current research focus of the Naya Lab. The Naya Lab is investigating the Dlk1-Dio3 ncRNA locus as a regulator of mitochondrial oxidative phosphorylation in skeletal muscle in a murine model.

To this end CRISPR-Cas9 induced promoter deletions were performed in C2C12 murine skeletal myoblasts resulting in two stable cell lines ΔMeg3-Pro and ΔMeg3-KO. A control cell line, pX was subjected to the Cas9 transfection without the guide RNAs. ΔMeg3-Pro represents a cell line with increased expression of Meg3 and ΔMeg3-KO represents a cell line with decreased expression of Meg3. ATAC-seq, RNA-seq and CUT&RUN analysis were performed on samples from these cell lines at two time points, proliferation and differentiation. Both the ATAC-seq and CUT and RUN data are accesible on the UCSC genome browser at the link below.
